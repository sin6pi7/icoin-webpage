Mission

Our mission is to increase financial awareness and promote entrepreneurship among IT specialists - especially students and former students of the IEiT faculty of the AGH University of Science and Technology.

We strive to achieve this goal by organizing lectures, workshops and engaging experts as well as entrepreneurs.

We are building a bridge to close the gap between the university campus and the world of business.

By doing this, we hope to fully realize our collective potential and build a better future, together.

We have already gathered more than 100 people.

About the meetings

"Financial ABC" is a series of introductory meetings about the worlds of finance and entrepreneurship. They aim to provide the most important knowledge, needed to fully understand this area, in the simplest way.

The lectures have been prepared and presented by members of iCoin Scientific Group.

INTRODUCTION TO MACROECONOMICS

Description

The first of the "Financial ABC" lectures introduces basic terms related to economics, as well as explains economical phenomenons that affect our lives.

Agenda

  • The genesis of money
  • Banks
  • Basic economical metrics
  • Inflation and deflation
  • Business cycle

FINANCIAL MATHEMATICS

Description

The lecture aims at explaining the most elementary terms related to financial mathematics. Understanding them is crucial to securing loans, assessing the profitability of a time deposit or calculating investment risk.

Agenda

  • Interest rates
  • Capitalization
  • Simple interest
  • Compound interest
  • Time deposits
  • Saving accounts
  • Loans
  • Real cost of loan

LABOUR LAW

Description

The third of the "Financial ABC" lectures covers different types of contracts signed between employees and employers. In particular, it points out the divergence in responsibilities and benefits that go along with each of them.

About the meetings

Finance is a field too broad to explore it at once. That's why it's so valuable to be in touch with experts specializing in it's various areas. By organizing these meetings, we're creating the opportunity and environment that allows students to make the most of this powerful source of knowledge.

REAL ESTATE INVESTMENT

About the lecture

The lecture, entitled "Earn Money Without Toil", presents an answer to the question, "What to do with excess money?" - one that usually appears in minds of well paid people at some point (including IT and Telecommunications professionals).

Dr Micha≈Ç Korzycki, who's been connecting research work with being an entrepreneur for the past 10 years, tells us about investing in real estate.

You'll learn about ways of obtaining passive income and the mechanisms of financial leverage.
You'll hear about risk, methods of increasing the value of your properties (also known as "home staging") as well as the legal and tax regulations that apply.
If that's not enough, you'll also be familiarized with the main principles of investment.
All of that combined with factual numbers and real-life examples.

Definitely the best value for your time that you could possibly find!

About the meetings

"From Zero to CEO" meetings bring about opportunity to create a connection between entrepreneurs and ambitious students of our faculty.
